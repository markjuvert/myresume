PUBLICATIONS

Using LSTM and ARIMA to Simulate and Predict Limestone Price Variations.

This was published in the Science Citation Index (SCI) during my PhD journey. The paper presents an implementation of two advanced deep neural networks (RNN and ARIMA) in simulation and predictions of varying patterns.
The paper involves a series of data collection, data treatment, data processing, parameter tuning, model creation, training and testing of the models, and so much more.Interestingly, all the steps and stages of the paper were committed to Github. Python Programming Language, and Jupyter Notebooks which were fundamental tools to help accomplish the project.

Implementation of Statistical Analysis to Determine Optimum Ultimate Pit.

This paper presents a heuristic approach in determining an ultimate value or point. Statistical analysis such as correlation analysis, K-means++ Analysis,Principal Component Analysis, and Generalized Linear models were used in the analysis of the models created.
Different models were created based on the Statistical analysis method and the results were displayed on diagrams to help in the interpretation of the results.
It is an interesting paper to read. One takeaway, how we manipulate and present data have a huge impact on how we interpret the results!
